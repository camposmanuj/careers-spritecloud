Who we are

At spriteCloud, testing is in our blood. Our software testing and automation consultancy company was founded by three testers in 2009, with the idea of creating a company that is a great place to work at for testers. Fast forward to 2022, we are now a team of 60+ and growing, with our headquarters in Amsterdam and an office in Barcelona, but the initial idea is still there.

No matter the size of spriteCloud, this sense of community that was so central to its creation should remain. This is not achieved by a top-down approach but by empowering our colleagues with the time, budget and flexibility to make spriteCloud the company they always wanted to work at.

As such, we encourage our staff to ask what they can do for spriteCloud rather than what spriteCloud can do for them. We have a shared responsibility to make spriteCloud a great place to work.

Our passion for testing and our commitment to high-quality work has allowed us to work with leading brands, both in the Netherlands and internationally, such as Heineken, Ahold and Adidas. For our staff, this means they get the incredible opportunity to develop their technical skills on interesting projects and build an impressive resumé of work experience.

Your Application Process

This is what you can expect when you apply to one of our job openings:

You Apply

We will check your CV and come back to you to plan a first interview if you are a suitable candidate.

1st Interview

In this first interview, we will talk about your skills and background to see if you are a fit to spriteCloud's culture

Assignment

If the first interview was a success we ask you to complete an assignment at home to assess your skills.

2nd Interview

We will discuss the result of the assignment and your technical skills during this second interview.

Contract Signing

Is it a match? We will make you an offer and get the ball rolling on your contract.
